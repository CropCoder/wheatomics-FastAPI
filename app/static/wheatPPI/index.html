Search wheat protein-protein interactions

About this data: The protein interaction data was downloaded from plant.MAP. We mapped wheat UniProt IDs to IWGSC RefSeq v1.1 annotation. According to McWhite et.al, CF-MS scores > 0.5 correspond to ~90% true positive rate; scores > 0.2 correspond to ~50% true positive rate.
Citation:
McWhite, C. D., Papoulas, O., Drew, K., Cox, R. M., June, V., Dong, O. X., Kwon, T., Wan, C., Salmi, M. L., Roux, S. J., Browning, K. S., Chen, Z. J., Ronald, P. C., & Marcotte, E. M. (2020). A Pan-plant Protein Complex Map Reveals Deep Conservation and Novel Assemblies. Cell, S0092-8674(20)30226-9.
https://doi.org/10.1016/j.cell.2020.02.049
